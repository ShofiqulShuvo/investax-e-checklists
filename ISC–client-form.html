Agency Agreement

By engaging with us, you appoint Investax Group, its related companies, or any of their directors, officers, employees, or licensees (“Investax Group”) as your agent for communication and correspondence with your chosen legal advisor to facilitate the provision of legal advice. Investax Group will maintain confidentiality of any communications or correspondence within the limits of the law and will use such information solely to further your requirements.
